How ShipCalc works

ShipCalc calculates the real cost of importing products from Japan, USA, China or Korea to any country in the world. Enter the purchase price in the local currency, the package weight and your destination — ShipCalc shows you the exact breakdown: shipping cost, customs duty, import VAT and platform commission if you plan to resell.

Exchange rates are updated in real time. Shipping rates cover Japan Post EMS, SAL, Air Small Packet and Surface, DHL, FedEx and UPS. Customs thresholds and import taxes are included for 20 destination countries including USA, Spain, Mexico, Argentina, Colombia, Chile, UK, Germany and Australia.

ShipCalc also offers a personal shipping service for travelers in Japan, Korea and China — we pick up your package, inspect the contents with you, and handle the full international shipping process so you can keep enjoying your trip.
